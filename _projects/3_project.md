[Awesome-World-Models](https://github.com/leofan90/Awesome-World-Models) is a research-oriented survey and resource index (Survey & Knowledge Base) for the World Model research direction.

## Overview

This project systematically curates representative works covering:

- **Video prediction** and generation
- **Action-conditioned dynamics** modeling
- **Embodied AI** applications
- **Reinforcement learning** with world models

The project provides a structured research entry point for the World Model and Embodied AI community, and is widely used for literature surveys, method comparisons, and research direction planning.

- Participated in maintaining and continuously expanding World Model related research resources
- Systematically organized representative works across multiple research directions
